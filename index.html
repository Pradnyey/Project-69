school

Was established in 2019

ARISE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL is a vision driven CBSE school committed to meet the educational needs of 21st century learners and has already made its footprints visible in the PCMC, Pune. We understand the educational needs of the young learners as those encompassing physical, social, intellectual and emotional development. To achieve the aspirations of young learners we have developed strong infrastructural base, comprehensive curriculum and technology driven delivery mechanism. This school has a very rich heritage and cultural inheritance in the form of JSPM (Latur) that boasts off 30+ years of vast experience in educating learners of various age groups

school library

Was built in 2019

AIS has an excellent and well set-up library. It consists of a large hall filled with shelves, bookstands and furniture. The room is airy and spacious. More than fifty students can use it at the same time. There are books on every subject including history, geography, science, civics, English language and literature, computer, general knowledge, etc. There are also a good number of reference books. There is the entire collection of Encyclopedia Britannica which is a real help for students. Each shelf contains a particular category of books. The stories’ section is the most sought after section of the library. Students can also borrow any book of the library with the permission of the librarian. The front section of the Library serves as Students Reading Room. It contains some newspapers and magazines. The teacher’s Reference section serves both as reading room as well as a room for reading up resource books. They instill in the young minds, the habit of reading, which will stand them in good stead in the future. The reference section helps them to clear their doubts while the reading room makes them conversant with the current affairs and day-to-day events of their country as well as of the whole world. Thus, a good library is an asset. It widens student’s mind, improves their knowledge, clears their doubts and instills in them the habit of reading. Libraries help to make learning a self-discovery and a personal venture.

Yoga

Was built in 2019

Yoga is a mind and body practice with a 5,000-year history in ancient Indian philosophy. Various styles of yoga combine physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation or relaxation. There are several different types of yoga and many disciplines within the practice. In the modern world, the South Asian art of yoga has expanded to all corners of the globe. While it is now a popular form of exercise and meditation. Arise students are made to practice meditation and yoga every day in the school assembly.
